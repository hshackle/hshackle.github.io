I am a current PhD student in physics at Harvard University, advised by Subir Sachdev. I am interested in a broad range of quantum many-body phenomena, including quantum criticality, topological order, and open quantum systems. I attended the Massachusetts Institute of Technology as an undergraduate, where I majored in both physics and philosophy. Starting in Fall 2024, I will be returning to MIT as a Pappalardo Fellow.

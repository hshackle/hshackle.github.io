I am a Pappalardo Fellow (2024-2027) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), interested in a broad range of quantum many-body phenomena including quantum criticality, topological order, and open quantum systems. I received my PhD in physics from Harvard University, advised by Subir Sachdev. Previously, I attended MIT as an undergraduate where I majored in both physics and philosophy.
